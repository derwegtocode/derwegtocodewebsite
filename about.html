About Me

Passionate web developer currently living in Spain. Learning german is my other passion, hence the name of this project. 'Der Weg' means 'The Way' in german, symbolizing that I'm on a path to becomimg a better developer.
Before getting fully immersed in the world of tech, I studied for 12 years in Argentina to become a doctor first, and then an anesthesiologist. I thoroughly enjoyed that part of my life, but I knew I wanted something else. As a very analytical person I take pleasure in solving problems, a fundamental part of being an anesthesiologist, but more often than not the creativity required to perform the job was limited.
Through coding I was able to combine both aspects, creating things by solving problems. So I decided to pursue this career to unleash my full potential. So far it has been challenging, but it has also been very rewarding.
As I was learning to develop websites I discovered Ruby and Ruby on Rails and fell in love with them. I hope I can keep growing as a developer and learning plenty of tools to outdo myself.
